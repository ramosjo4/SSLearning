We are very proud to offer healthy, delicious meals and snacks to our students. We get all our meals from The Café @The Commons, Chef Jeremiah Lynch has created a kid friendly menu for all our students. Parents receive our weekly menu in advance so we can accommodate to any allergy issues. Our half day students get a daily snack and full day students get two snacks and lunch every day; all these healthy options are included in our tuition. To learn more about our menu or about Chef Jeremiah visit Café at The Commons on Facebook or go to Cafe @ The Commons.
